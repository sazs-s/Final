Top 10 Places to Visit

1. Chennai

Chennai is the capital of Tamil Nadu. It is famous for Marina Beach, the second longest beach in the world, and beautiful temples like Kapaleeshwarar.

2. Madurai

Madurai is one of the oldest cities in India. The Meenakshi Amman Temple is a world-famous landmark known for its tall, colorful towers.

Start Navigation

3. Ooty

Ooty is a beautiful hill station in the Nilgiris. It is famous for its tea gardens, cool weather, and the historic toy train ride.

Start Navigation

4. Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is called the "Princess of Hill Stations." It has a lovely star-shaped lake, pine forests, and many scenic waterfalls.

Start Navigation

5. Mahabalipuram

This seaside town is a UNESCO Heritage site. It is famous for its rock-cut temples and ancient stone carvings from the Pallava period.

Start Navigation

6. Thanjavur

Thanjavur is the home of the Great Brihadeeswarar Temple. It is also famous for its unique Tanjore paintings and classical music.

Start Navigation

7. Rameswaram

An island town connected by the famous Pamban Bridge. It is a major pilgrimage site with a temple that has the longest corridor in the world.

Start Navigation

8. Kanyakumari

The southern tip of India where three seas meet. It is the best place to watch the sunrise and sunset over the ocean.

Start Navigation

9. Coimbatore

A big industrial city near the mountains. It is the gateway to the Nilgiris and home to the giant Adiyogi Shiva statue.

Start Navigation

10. Tiruchirappalli

Also known as Trichy, it is famous for the Rockfort Temple built on a massive rock and the Srirangam temple complex.
